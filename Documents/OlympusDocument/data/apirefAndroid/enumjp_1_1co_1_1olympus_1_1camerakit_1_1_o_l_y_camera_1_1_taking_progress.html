Member Data Documentation

BeginCapturing

Start exposure.

This timing is appropriate for application to play a sound effect or display that the shutter is open.

BeginFocusing

Start auto focus.

This notification is called even if focus is locked.

EndCapturing

Exposure is complete.

This timing is appropriate for application to play a sound effect or display that the shutter is closed.

EndFocusing

End auto focus.

This timing is appropriate for application to play a sound effect or display the focus result.
